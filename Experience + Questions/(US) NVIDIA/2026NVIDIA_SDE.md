## Detailed Interview

The NVIDIA SDE Summer Internship 2026 process started with an Online Assessment and then moved to an interview round. The overall process was not only about coding. It also tested computer science basics, math, and logical thinking.

## Online Assessment

The first step was a HackerRank Online Assessment.

### Duration

- 1.5 hours

### Format

The assessment included:

- Two medium coding problems
- Sixteen MCQs
- Logical reasoning questions
- Brain teasers

### Topics Covered

- Arrays
- Algorithms
- Object-Oriented Programming
- Operating Systems
- DBMS
- Logical reasoning

### Key Takeaway

Do not only practice LeetCode. Core CS subjects are also important for this round.

## Interview Round

The interview was scheduled around five days after the Online Assessment.

### Platform

- Microsoft Teams

### Duration

- 1.5 hours

### Format

The interview had a panel of three interviewers. Each interviewer focused on a different area.

## Interviewer 1: Coding

The first interviewer asked which language the candidate was most comfortable with. The candidate answered C++.

The interviewer then gave a subarray sum problem. The candidate first explained the brute-force approach and then moved to the optimized solution.

The interviewer asked follow-up questions during the explanation. This part took around 30 minutes.

### Key Takeaway

Be ready to explain your logic clearly. It is important to show both the simple approach and the optimized approach.

## Interviewer 2: Mathematics

The second interviewer focused on math.

### Topics Discussed

- Sets
- Union
- Intersection
- Related discrete math concepts

The interviewer gave hints when the candidate got stuck, but the questions still required careful thinking.

### Key Takeaway

Brush up on discrete math before the interview.

## Interviewer 3: Logic Puzzle

The third interviewer gave a logic puzzle based on password detection from a set of clues.

The candidate had to use the clues step by step and find the correct password.

### Key Takeaway

Practice basic logic puzzles and explain your thinking while solving.

## General Discussion

After the three sections, there was a general discussion. The interviewers also gave time for the candidate to ask questions.

## Final Result

The final result was not specified in the shared experience.

## Key Takeaways

- NVIDIA interviews can test more than coding.
- Practice arrays and algorithmic problems.
- Revise OOP, OS, and DBMS basics.
- Review discrete math, especially sets, union, and intersection.
- Be comfortable explaining brute-force and optimized solutions.
- Practice logic puzzles and brain teasers.
- Think out loud during the interview.
